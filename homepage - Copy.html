AMPD '50th Anniversary' Creative Campaign

Set of postcards representing the various departments within the School of the Arts, Media, Performance & Design.

Business Objectives:

  • To create and design a creative campaign for guests attending the celebration of 50 years of the School of the Arts, Media, Performance and Design (AMPD).

  • To create a campaign that takes a look back on the 50 years of history, creativity and transformation of AMPD.

  • To create memorable items that promote the faculty, reach out to alumni, and encourage more people to support arts education.

Designed by Robyn Chmelyk, Elyssa Biringer & Ashton Frohn.
